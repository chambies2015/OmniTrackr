Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Use the "Forgot password" link on the sign-in page. You will receive an email with instructions to set a new password.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. You can deactivate your account in account settings. For full deletion of your data, contact us by email.

Do you offer paid features?

OmniTrackr is free to use. We offer an optional Ko-fi page if you want to support the project financially.

Where can I read about privacy and terms?

See our Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions for full details.
