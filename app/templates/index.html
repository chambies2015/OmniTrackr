Frequently Asked Questions

OmniTrackr is a free, web-based application that helps you track and organize your movie, TV show, and anime collection. You can rate, review, and analyze your media with comprehensive statistics, beautiful posters, and social features to share with friends.

Yes! OmniTrackr is completely free to use. There are no subscription fees, premium tiers, or hidden costs. Just create an account and start tracking your media collection.

Simply use the "Add Movie", "Add TV Show", or "Add Anime" forms in your dashboard. Enter the title, year, and other details. The app will automatically fetch beautiful posters from the OMDB API. You can also import your collection from JSON files.

Yes! You can export your entire collection (movies, TV shows, and anime) as a JSON file at any time. This allows you to backup your data or import it into another account. You can also import JSON files to quickly add multiple entries.

You can send friend requests to other users by their username. When someone sends you a request, you'll receive a notification. You can accept or deny requests. Once accepted, you can view each other's collections and statistics (respecting privacy settings). Friend requests expire after 30 days if not responded to.

Privacy settings allow you to control what friends can see. You can make your movies, TV shows, anime, or statistics private. When enabled, even your friends won't be able to see that data - it's completely private to you. You can change these settings anytime in your account settings.

Click on your username in the top right corner to open the account settings modal. From there, you can change your username, email, password, upload a profile picture, and manage your privacy settings. Email changes require verification via email.

Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Click the link in the email to set a new password. Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't see the email.

Yes! You can rate any movie, TV show, or anime from 0 to 10.0 with one decimal place precision (e.g., 7.5, 8.4, 9.2). The statistics dashboard will calculate averages and other metrics using these decimal ratings.

You can reach us via email at omnitrackr@gmail.com. You can also find us on GitHub or LinkedIn. We're happy to help!
